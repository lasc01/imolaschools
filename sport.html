Sport and Game Week


Sports play a great role in our lives because it keeps us healthy and active. We have a healthy mind only
when we have a healthy body. Sport raises self-esteem, develops freedom, makes a person stronger morally
than he or she has ever been.

The importance of sports and games in school comprise more than just the benefit of physical activity.
Increase in self-respect and mental watchfulness make school sports and games necessary for every
school age child.

At Imola Schools, we lay so much empasis on sport and games, as we believe that every child needs to be.
physically and mentally fit for real life activities. Due to this, we organize a sport & game week every
session to create a sense of competition among our students.
