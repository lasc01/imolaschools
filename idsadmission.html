Primary school Admission

Admission Procedure and Requirements

1. Acquire our admission form for free at the school.
2. Submit the filled form with two (2) passport photographs,one (1) photocopy of Birth
Certificate and Health Form.
3. The parents or guardian of the child will be interviewed to get certain information
about the child.
4. Your child will take a test for class placement(For pupils from Nursery 1 above).
5. Admission letter and other documents will be issued after the result of the test is out.
6. Commence with the payment of the school fees at the bank or at the Bursar's office
(Bank: Sterling bank, Account name: Imola Comprehensive College, Account Number:
0030081654)
7. School uniform and other school material is then issued to your child to kick off as a
pupil of Imola Day School.
