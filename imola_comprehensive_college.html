Welcome Address

On behalf of the management, staffs and students of Imola schools, I welcome you to our official website.
Imola school is a Co-educational school founded on 12th July, 1969. Our schools are situated at 4,
Adewumi Ogefon Street,Alasia-Oshodi and 83, Iju Road, Ifako Agege, Lagos respectively.

Imola school is committed to helping students develop their skills and talents through a wide range of
exposure to numerous learning experiences. We are also interested in the physical, social and psychological
development of our Students, edging them to become responsible and impact driven members of the society.

We pride ourselves at offering a robust curriculum that is geared towards bringing out excellence from our
students. Emphasis is on National curriculum. Using their professional expertise, the school management and
teaching staffs deliver a robust scheme of work in various subjects based on the curriculum.

Our facilities are up-to-date making us infra-structurally positioned to deliver quality education to
your children's applications from all parts of the country.

Connect with us via imolaschools@gmail.com.
We look forward to having a rewarding relationship with you.
